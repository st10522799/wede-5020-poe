## Project Overview

**Background of organisation:** Espresso café was created to make coffee culture accessible to everyday South Africans and focusing on low-cost drinks and pastries(croissants, muffins, donuts and pies) but
                            lastly fast service for people on the go. It became popular for offering affordable, quick coffee and baked goods at a fixed price point of R14 for every item.

**Vision:** We envision a future where everyone can enjoy high-quality coffee and fresh baked goods at accessible prices to grow the brand while maintaining commitment to affordability, efficiency and customer
        satisfaction
        
**Mission:** We strive to provide fast, friendly service in convenient locations ensuring a nice experience while creating a welcoming environment.

**Target audience:** •	Students 
                     •	Commuters
                     •	Workers needing quick breakfast

## Website Goals and Objectives

The goal of the espresso café website is to : •	Promote the brand as a leading affordable coffee provider
                                              •	Provide essential information about its affordable coffee and pastry offerings
                                              
 The objectives include: •	Increasing Store visits
                         •	Improving customer awareness
                         •	Delivering a user-friendly experience that allows customers to easily access menu, location and contact details.
